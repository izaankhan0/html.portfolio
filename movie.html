My Top 3 Movies of all time

1. Spiderman Across the Spider verse

This movie had a wonderful story line and its own unique way of portraying a story which was so diverse and not restricted to any limit of imaginable possibilities

2. The Unlisted (series)

Although the ending was a little wacky, this series had an excellent mix of comedy and a catching and unique sotry line that seems straight out of my 5 year self's imagination while keeping it in touch with reality

3. Cobra Kai

A series which had an amazing hook and an ability to create a sense of mystery also having a touch of a good comedy script while some things are questionable in it
